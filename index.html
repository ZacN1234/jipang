Our Story

Started With a Dream and a Pot of Broth

Jipang is a family-run Japanese home-style kitchen that has been serving the Manly community from The Corso since 1993 — over 30 years of cooking with love.

"Nothing can beat mum's cooking but our home-style kitchen is pretty close to it because we cook every meal with extra love."

In 1993, the Jipang family opened a tiny kitchen on The Corso with a handful of tables and a dream of sharing real Japanese home cooking with the Manly community. Three decades later, the recipes haven't changed — because when something works, you don't fix it.

You'll notice it the moment you sit down. The regulars who wave to the kitchen. The staff who remember your order. The smell of sesame oil and dashi that hits you at the door. This isn't a chain restaurant — it's someone's family feeding yours, one bowl at a time. Jipang is proud to be part of Manly's dining scene on Sydney's Northern Beaches.

Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions About Jipang Manly

Everything you need to know about visiting Jipang on The Corso, Manly.

We are located at Shop 9, 37-39 The Corso, Manly NSW 2095 — right in the heart of Manly's main pedestrian strip, just steps from Manly Beach and Manly Wharf. You will find us on The Corso, the Northern Beaches' favourite walkway.

We are open 7 days a week from 11:30am to 8:00pm. Come in any day for lunch or dinner!

We are located on The Corso, which is a pedestrian mall. The closest parking options include the Manly Council Car Park on Whistler Street and metered street parking on surrounding streets. Manly is also easily accessible by ferry from Circular Quay.

We are fully licensed with a selection of Japanese beers, sake and soft drinks. We are also BYO wine only — so feel free to bring your favourite bottle!

Yes! We have plenty of vegetarian and vegan options on our menu. Try our Teriyaki Vege and Tofu, Vege Tempura, Veg Fried Rice, Teriyaki Tofu and Vege Don, or our Plain Udon and Vege Tempura Udon which both have vegan options available. Our Agedashi Tofu also has a vegan option.

Yes, we offer delivery through UberEats and DoorDash. You can also order online for pickup through our website, or call us on (02) 9977 4436.

We accept group bookings for parties of 5 or more, with 90-minute seatings available at 5:00pm or 6:30pm. For smaller groups, just walk in — we would love to see you!

Yes! We have a kids menu for children under 12 with Kids Udon ($15) and Kids Bento Box ($18). We are a family-friendly restaurant and love having little ones visit.

We offer lunch specials Monday to Friday from 11:30am to 2:30pm, available for both eat-in and takeaway. Please note these are not available on public holidays.

Yes, we offer student specials on Monday and Tuesday all day for eat-in only. These are not available on public holidays.

We are a Japanese home-style kitchen. Our menu includes ramen, udon, Japanese curry, rice bowls (don), bento boxes, teriyaki dishes, tempura, gyoza, takoyaki, karaage and much more. Everything is made fresh to order using our family recipes.

Our customers love our chicken katsu curry, gyoza, ramen (especially the chashu and curry ramen), bento boxes, and takoyaki. The teriyaki dishes and salmon poke don are also favourites. Come in and try them for yourself!

Yes, we are wheelchair accessible. Everyone is welcome at Jipang.

We accept Cash, Visa, American Express (Amex) and MasterCard.

Most of our mains, ramen, udon, rice bowls and curries are between $20 and $27. Starters range from $4 to $17, and our bento box is $33. We're known for being great value for Japanese food in Manly.

We don't have a specific gluten-free menu, but some of our dishes can be adapted. Please let our staff know about any allergies or dietary requirements when you order and we'll do our best to help.

Absolutely! You can order online for pickup through our website, call us on (02) 9977 4436, or order delivery through UberEats and DoorDash. Our lunch specials are available for takeaway too.
