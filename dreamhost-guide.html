A quick guide for jipang-manly.com

To get your new website live on your domain, we just need to point your domain to a new hosting system (Cloudflare). This takes about 5 minutes and I'll make sure everything — including your email — keeps working perfectly.

I'll set up all your email records (MX, SPF, DKIM) on the new system before asking you to make this change. Your email will keep working without interruption. I won't ask you to switch until everything is ready.

Step-by-Step for DreamHost

1
Go to panel.dreamhost.com and log in to your account
2
In the left sidebar, click "Domains" then "Registrations"
3
Click the "Modify Whois" link (or the wrench icon) next to jipang-manly.com
4
Scroll down to the "Nameservers" section
5
Select "Use another host's nameservers" (not DreamHost's)
6
Delete all 4 current AWS nameservers
7
Enter the two new Cloudflare nameservers:
  • dave.ns.cloudflare.com
  • shubhi.ns.cloudflare.com
8
Click "Save Nameservers" to apply the changes

Changes usually take 1–24 hours to take effect (often much faster). During this time, your website may show the old or new version intermittently — that's completely normal.
